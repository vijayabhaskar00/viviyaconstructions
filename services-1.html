Heritage Buildings

Older, culturally significant buildings are an important part of the built environment. They are part of our history and they enrich our towns, cities and suburbs. Restoring and Constructing older buildings is therefore important. It is specialist work, though, so you need a specialist contractor.

We have experience working on a range of building and restoration projects so understand the specific requirements, challenges, and expectations of this type of work. In addition, the quality of work we deliver is second to none, as is our attention to detail. Construction and restoration of heritage building requires unique technical skills which are in extinction and our company adopted these skills from dying generations and rebuilding authentic and unique buildings where lot of details, art and science are involved - A niche market and niche contractors with highly skilled team is Viva Construction.
